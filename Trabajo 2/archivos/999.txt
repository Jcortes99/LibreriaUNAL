The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes
The Adventures of Sherlock Holmesis the series of short stories that made the fortunes of the Strand magazine, in which they were first published, and won immense popularity for Sherlock Holmes and Dr Watson. The detective is at the height of his powers and the volume is full of famous cases, including 'The Red-Headed League', 'The Blue Carbuncle', and 'The Speckled Band'.The editor of this volume, Richard Lancelyn Green is editor ofThe Uncollected Sherlock HolmesandThe Further Adventures of Sherlock Holmes. With John Michael Gibson, he compiled the Soho Series Bibliography of A. Conan Doyle.
